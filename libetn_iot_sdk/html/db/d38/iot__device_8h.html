IOT_UNKNOWN

IOT_CLOSING PENDING_CLOSE UNAUTHENTICATED ?
IOT_CLOSED NOT_CONNECTED DISCONNECTED or ERROR UNAUTHENTICATED (the rest)
IOT_CONNECTING CONNECTING CONNECTING UNAUTHENTICATED NO_NETWORK
