enum eds_ext_cmd_enum_t

Enum set for extra non-Modbus "Commands" Some of these will need "special" handling, often with hard-coded handling for cases too difficult to express in general EDS terms (eg, Waveforms).

Assigning these a range of 0x7100-7FFF, assuming that no INCOM command (eg, 3xx_yyy) will ever fall in this range
