void px::asio::CIoActor::close ( )
virtual

Gracefully close this actor.

This will gracefully close the actor. It is called automatically by the actor's destructor, but any resulting error information will be lost in that case.

Exceptions
boost::system::system_errorThere was an error when attempting to gracefully shut down / close
