#define ITAG_REALTIMESDISABLED   12662

Manage the identifying information for our "special", internal-use-only "channels" that manage IoT publishing for this device.

SetChannelValues and GetChannelValues can list these as though they were real channels, but they are not found in the device profiles and are not sent to the CDS as channels of the device. Instead, the values are held internally by this class, and non-default values can be saved in the IoT Device Settings (and restored on reboot).

We will list the numeric iTags and the names of these "channels"; we will use the names as the parameter label when saving to the OOB. The little array here will associate the iTags with those names.
